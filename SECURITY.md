# Security Policy

## Reporting a vulnerability

If you believe you have found a security issue in an `andzie-co` repo, **do not open a public issue.**

Instead, open a private vulnerability report via GitHub:

1. Go to the affected repository.
2. Click **Security** → **Report a vulnerability**.
3. Fill in the form with as much detail as you can share.

We will acknowledge within 3 business days and respond with a triage assessment.

## Scope

- All repositories under the `andzie-co` GitHub organization.
- Deployed services operated by Andzie Holding Company.

## Out of scope

- Third-party dependencies (report those to the upstream project).
- Social engineering of employees.
- Physical security.

## Safe harbor

We will not pursue legal action against good-faith research that:

- Respects user privacy and does not exfiltrate data.
- Avoids service degradation or data destruction.
- Gives us reasonable time to respond before public disclosure.
